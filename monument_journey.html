Three scenarios you might hit
✅ Normal login - returning device
Your device is recognised → biometrics or PIN → we fetch your latest account data in the background → straight into the app. Takes seconds.
📱 New device
Your old phone gets a push notification asking "was this you?" → approve it → new phone asks you to set PIN + biometrics → you're in on the new device too.
🔑 Forgotten PIN
Tap "Forgotten PIN" → SMS code sent to your registered number → 2 minutes to enter it → reset your PIN → back in immediately.
Lockout rules: 5 wrong PINs in 30 min → 30-min lockout. 5 wrong OTPs → 60-min lockout. 3 wrong memorable word attempts → 1-min lockout. Face ID / fingerprint can unlock a PIN lockout caused by biometric failures.

These all run silently. You only see something if a check fails.

🔍 Jailbreak / Root check
Runs on every app open. Jailbroken iPhone or rooted Android? Hard block. "Access not allowed on modified devices." No way around it.
🤖 Fraud scoring (RiskID)
Silent AI risk check before every sensitive action. Returns ALLOW or DENY. DENY stops the flow immediately, no explanation given to the user.
📱 Device binding
Your phone is cryptographically paired to your account. A stranger with your password on a new device still can't log in without approving on your trusted phone.
🔗 Secure step tokens
When you move between steps in a flow, we use encrypted short-lived tokens (15 min expiry). Nothing sensitive sits in a URL or gets passed in plain text.
📞 SMS OTP
6 digits, expires in 2 minutes. Only wrong attempts count against your limit - accidentally tapping the wrong digit and correcting it doesn't penalise you.
🧠 Memorable word
An optional extra layer with a hint shown to jog your memory. Used in high-risk account recovery. 3 failures triggers a temporary lockout.

✉️ Email verify
1 - Email check
Already a Monument customer with this email? Shown an information screen and stopped - no duplicate accounts. If new, a secure token is created to carry your email safely to step 2.
2 - Phone verification
6-digit SMS code to your mobile. Proves you own the number. The step-1 token is consumed and a new one is issued - now carrying your verified email AND verified phone together.
3 - Risk assessment
Silent fraud check. DENY = application is stopped here. ALLOW = you move to account creation. You don't see this step at all if everything is fine.
4 - Account created
Your record is created in Monument's systems. You receive a unique partyId - your permanent identity across everything at Monument. A signed token is returned to the app to confirm success.
5 - Email verification
Verification email sent. Your identity verification flag is marked complete. You're now a full Monument member with an active savings account.
